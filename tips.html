Plant Care Tips

🌼 Popular Houseplants

  • 🪴 Water your cactus once every 2-3 weeks. Let the soil dry out completely between waterings.
  • 🌿 Ferns love moisture. Mist them daily and keep them in indirect light.
  • 🐍 Snake plants thrive on neglect. Water lightly and avoid overwatering.

💚 Healthy Plants

  • 🧴 Aloe Vera prefers bright, indirect light. Water deeply but infrequently.
  • 🌱 Basil needs at least 6 hours of sunlight. Keep the soil moist but not soggy.
  • 🌿 Neem grows best in full sunlight and needs regular pruning to stay bushy.

🌬️ Air-Purifying Plants

  • ✿ Peace lilies need consistent moisture and shaded light to bloom well.
  • 🌴 Areca palms need humidity and bright, filtered light. Wipe leaves to keep clean.
  • 🌳 Rubber plants need indirect light and should be rotated weekly for even growth.

🪴 Low Maintenance Plants

  • 🕶️ ZZ Plants tolerate low light and infrequent watering—perfect for beginners.
  • 🌿 Pothos thrives in low light and grows fast—cut and propagate easily.
  • 💧 Jade plants store water in leaves—water only when soil is dry.

🌿 Regular Everyday Plants

  • 🌱 Tulsi (Holy Basil): Needs regular watering and sunlight. Very beneficial for health and air quality.
  • 🍃 Mint: Requires partial sunlight and plenty of water. Great for kitchen gardens.
  • 🌿 Curry Leaves: Grows well in warm climates. Needs frequent watering and full sun.
